# 📢 Marketing & Copywriting Prompts

A curated collection of reusable prompt templates for creating high-converting marketing and copywriting content using AI.

---

## 1. Ad Copy Generator

**Use Case:** Creating advertisements

**Prompt:**

```text id="ad91kp"
Act as a professional copywriter.

Write high-converting ad copy for [PRODUCT/SERVICE].

Include:
- Headline  
- Problem  
- Solution  
- Benefits  
- Call to action  

Tone: Persuasive and engaging
```

**Inputs:**

* Product/Service

**Output:**

* Ad copy

**Variations:**

* Short ad copy
* Social media ads

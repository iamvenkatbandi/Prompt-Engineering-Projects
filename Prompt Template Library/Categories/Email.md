# 📧 Email Drafting Prompts

A curated collection of reusable prompt templates for generating professional, clear, and effective emails using AI tools like ChatGPT and Gemini.

---

## 1. Cold Email

**Use Case:** Reaching out to potential clients or prospects

**Prompt:**

```text
Act as a professional email copywriter.

Write a cold email to [TARGET AUDIENCE] offering [SERVICE/PRODUCT].

Include:
- Personalized opening  
- Problem statement  
- Value proposition  
- Clear call to action  

Tone: Professional and persuasive
```

**Inputs:**

* Target audience
* Service/Product

**Output:**

* Cold email

**Variations:**

* Short cold email
* Personalized cold outreach

---

## 2. Follow-Up Email

**Use Case:** Following up after no response

**Prompt:**

```text
Write a polite follow-up email regarding [SITUATION].

Include:
- Reference to previous email  
- Gentle reminder  
- Call to action  

Keep it short and professional.
```

**Inputs:**

* Situation

**Output:**

* Follow-up email

**Variations:**

* Formal follow-up
* Friendly follow-up

## 3. Job Application Email

**Prompt:**

```text
- Relevant skills and experience

- Closing statement  

Tone: Professional and confident
```

**Inputs:**

* Job role

**Output:**

* Job application email

**Variations:**

* Fresher application

# Content Writing Prompts
---
## 1. Blog Writer
**Use Case:** Writing detailed blog posts
**Prompt:**
```text
Act as an expert content writer.

Task: Write a blog post on [TOPIC]

Audience: [AUDIENCE]

Include:
- Introduction
- Subheadings
- Examples
- Conclusion
```
**Inputs:**
- Topic
- Audience

**Output:**
- Full blog article

**Variations:**
- SEO blog
- Short blog
